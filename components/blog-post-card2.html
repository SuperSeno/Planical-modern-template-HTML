Phones in class

The essay argues that cell phones should be allowed in classrooms because they can provide students with access to useful tools that would be expensive to obtain otherwise, help with communication between students, teachers, and parents, and enhance the enjoyment of education through various tools. Despite concerns about distraction, cheating, and cyberbullying, the author believes that proper guidance can address these issues. The essay emphasizes that cell phone use should be moderated and should not be mandatory, and that cell phones can make learning more enjoyable and informative.
